The Contact Hours Paradox

Teachers Are the First Line of Defense

Students spend more waking hours with teachers than with parents during the school week. Yet most teachers receive zero mental health training.

  • 1,000+ Hours Annually

    Teachers interact with students daily, making them uniquely positioned to notice behavioral changes and warning signs.

  • Trusted Relationships

    Students often confide in teachers before parents. Teachers need the skills to respond appropriately when they do.

  • Zero Formal Training

    Most teacher education programs include no mental health curriculum, leaving teachers unprepared for crisis situations.
